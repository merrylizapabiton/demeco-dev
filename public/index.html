A Web-based Online Fuel Ordering and Delivery System designed with the specific goal of helping people who ran out of gas in the middle of their trip, and make it available to everyone with an Internet connection wherever. And will also give it to them quickly. The application is intended to make obtaining fuel easier and more efficient. Customers can order fuel online and have it delivered to their home or office using this web-based technology. By providing a web-based ordering system that eliminates the inconveniences associated with old methods, our project intends to transform how individuals and companies source fuel.
